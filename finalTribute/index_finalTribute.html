Paul of Tarsus

Ministry to the Gentiles

Here are places Paul took the Gospel to:

  • First Journey - Antioch, Asia Minor, Perga and back to Antioch
  • Second Journey - Jeruselum and around the Mediterranean
  • Third Journey - Around the region of Galatia and Phrygia
  • Other Journeys - At least 5 missionary journeys
  • Death - Paul was martyred by beheading
